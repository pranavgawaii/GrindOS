1. 2D Mensuration (Area & Perimeter)

ShapeAreaPerimeter / Circumference
Triangle1/2 * base * height (or Heron's formula)a + b + c
Equilateral Triangle(√3/4) * a²3a
Circleπ * r²2 * π * r
RectangleLength * Width2 * (Length + Width)

2. 3D Mensuration (Volume & Surface Area)

ShapeVolumeTotal Surface Area (TSA)
Cubea³6 * a²
Cylinderπ * r² * h2 * π * r * (r + h)
Cone1/3 * π * r² * hπ * r * (l + r) [l = √(r² + h²)]
Sphere4/3 * π * r³4 * π * r²

💡 Placement Shortcut: Percentage Dimension Changes

If radius of a sphere/circle increases by x%, the area/surface area increases by 2x + (x²/100)%.
