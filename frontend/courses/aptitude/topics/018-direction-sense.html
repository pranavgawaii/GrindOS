1. Direction Sense Basics

Always refer to the standard 8-direction cardinal map:

                 North (N)
                    │
         NW         │         NE
           \        │        /
            \       │       /
  West (W) ─────────┼───────── East (E)
            /       │                  /        │                 SW         │         SE
                    │
                 South (S)

2. Turns and Angles

  • A right turn is always a **90° clockwise** turn.
  • A left turn is always a **90° counter-clockwise** turn.

3. Shadow Problems

  • In Morning (Sunrise in East): Shadows always fall in the **West**.
  • In Evening (Sunset in West): Shadows always fall in the **East**.
  • At Noon (12 PM): No shadow.

💡 Shortcut: Pythagoras Theorem

To find the shortest distance from the starting point, construct a right-angled triangle and apply: Hypotenuse = √(Base² + Height²).
